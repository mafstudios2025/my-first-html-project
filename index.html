There are many diffrient type of fantasy sub genres

  • urban fantasy

    • urban fantasy is a subgenre of fantasy that takes place in a contemporary, real-world, often urban (city) setting, but introduces supernatural elements into that familiar environment.

  • science fantasy

    • Science fantasy is a subgenre of speculative fiction that blends elements and tropes from both science fiction and fantasy. It creates worlds where advanced technology, scientific principles, and often futuristic settings coexist with magical, mythical, or supernatural phenomena.

  • techno fantasy

    • Techno-fantasy is a subgenre of fantasy that incorporates elements of science and technology without necessarily providing scientific or pseudo-scientific rationalizations for their existence or function. This is the key differentiator from science fantasy.

  • dark fantasy

    • Dark fantasy is a subgenre of fantasy that leans heavily into disturbing, grim, and often horrific themes and aesthetics. While all fantasy can have its dark moments, dark fantasy makes the unsettling and morally ambiguous its central focus.
